Pancakes

Pancakes are soft, fluffy cakes made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter, cooked on a griddle or pan. They‚Äôre often served stacked and topped with syrup, fruit, or other sweet or savory additions.

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1¬Ω to 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted and cooled butter (optional), plus unmelted butter for cooking, or use neutral oil

Instructions:

  2. In a bowl, mix together dry ingredients.
  3. Beat eggs into 1¬Ω cups milk, then stir in 2 tablespoons melted cooled butter, if using it.

Fruit salad

Fruit salad is a refreshing mix of colorful, juicy fruits combined into a light and healthy dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 kiwi
  • 1 mango
  • 150 g pineapple
  • 100 g grapes
  • 400 g mixed berries (strawberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 1 large orange
  • 2 teaspoons honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the kiwi: cut off top and bottom, peel, slice in half, then cut into slices. Place in a bowl and repeat with the second kiwi.
  2. Carefully peel the mango, slice off each cheek close to the stone, cut into slices, and add to the kiwi.
  3. Top and tail the pineapple, peel, remove the eyes in a spiral pattern, then cut about 150 g into chunks. Add to the bowl.
  4. Halve the grapes and add them along with the berries. Slice or halve strawberries if large.
  5. Peel the orange, segment it over the bowl so the juice falls in, squeeze the remaining membrane for extra juice, and drizzle with honey if desired.
  6. Mix everything together and refrigerate for 30 minutes to macerate before serving.

Hamburger

A hamburger is a savory sandwich made with a seasoned ground beef patty, served in a bun with toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb ground chuck beef (80/20)
  • Fine sea salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 6 burger buns
  • 6 slices medium cheddar cheese (thick sliced)
  • 1 large tomato, sliced into 8 rings
  • 12 leaves green leaf lettuce
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced into rings
  • 1/2 cup dill pickle slices

Instructions:

  1. Prepare all burger toppings and sauces in advance.
  2. Toast buns: spread cut-side with softened or melted butter and toast on a skillet or grill until golden brown.
  3. Preheat grill to medium heat. Divide ground beef into 6 portions (about 1/3 lb each) and form patties about 1/2" thick and 1" wider than the buns.
  4. Season patties generously with salt and pepper just before grilling.
  5. Grill patties (indent-side-up) covered for 3‚Äì5 minutes per side. Flip when juices accumulate on top and sear is achieved.
  6. Apply cheese in the last 1‚Äì2 minutes of grilling, cover with lid, and cook until beef reaches 155¬∞F (final temperature 160¬∞F after resting).
  7. Rest patties for 5 minutes before serving.
  8. Assemble burgers: spread sauce on buns, layer pickles, lettuce, tomato, and onion, then place cheesy patty and top bun.

    Mixed berry muffins

    Mixed berry muffins are soft, golden treats bursting with juicy berries, offering a sweet and tangy flavor in every bite

    Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (or sour cream)
  • 1/2 cup extra light olive oil (not extra virgin)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup strawberries, hulled and diced
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries

    • Instructions:

    1. Line a 12-count muffin tin with cupcake liners and preheat oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C).
    2. In a large mixing bowl, beat 2 eggs with 1 cup sugar on high speed for 5 minutes until thick and light in color.
    3. Add Greek yogurt, olive oil, and vanilla. Mix on low speed until just combined.
    4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
    5. Add flour mixture into the batter in thirds, stirring gently each time. Do not overmix.
    6. Fold in strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries with a spatula until just combined.
    7. Divide batter evenly into muffin cups, filling to the top.
    8. Bake in the center of the oven for 20‚Äì22 minutes, until tops are golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
    9. Transfer muffins to a wire rack to cool. Serve warm or at room temperature.

    Chocolate milk

    Chocolate milk is a creamy, sweet drink made by blending milk with cocoa and sugar for a rich flavor

    Ingredients:

  • 4 pitted Medjool dates (soaked if dry)

    Stir Fry

    Stir fry is a quick, flavorful dish made by cooking bite-sized ingredients over high heat with minimal oil

    Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 zucchini, sliced or cubed
  • 2 tablespoons oil, divided
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small carrot, julienned or cubed
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 red pepper, cubed
  • 1/2 onion, cubed
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/2 cup cashews

    • Instructions:

    1. Trim chicken thighs of excess fat and cut into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Cut vegetables into even-sized pieces.
    3. Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
    4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    5. Add chicken in a single layer, cook until browned and cooked through, then remove from pan.
    6. Add remaining oil and butter to the pan, then add broccoli, zucchini, mushrooms, red pepper, onion, and carrots. Cook until crisp-tender.
    7. Return chicken to the pan. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
    8. Add cashews, then pour in the sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens and coats the chicken and vegetables.
    9. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

      10. Curry rice

      Curry rice is a fragrant dish where rice is cooked with spices like curry powder, ginger, and paprika, often combined with vegetables for a warm, flavorful meal

      Ingredients:

      • 1 tablespoon olive oil
      • 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
      • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
      • 3/4 teaspoon salt
      • 3/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
      • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
      • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
      • 2 teaspoons curry powder
      • 1 1/2 cups frozen peas
      • 2 carrots, grated
      • 1 cup long-grain rice
      • 2 cups vegetable stock (or broth)

      Instructions:

      1. Peel and finely chop the onion and garlic.
      2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
      3. Add onion, garlic, salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, ginger, and curry powder. Mix well and cook for 3‚Äì4 minutes.
      4. Grate the carrots and add them to the pan along with frozen peas. Stir and cook for another 2‚Äì3 minutes.
      5. Add rice and pour in vegetable stock. Mix well again.
      6. Cover the pan and cook for 15 minutes over medium-low heat.
      7. When done, transfer to serving plates.
      8. Optionally top with finely chopped cashews or cilantro and garnish with lime wedges.
      9. Serve warm and enjoy!
